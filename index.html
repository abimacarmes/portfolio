About Me

I've always been interested in web development but never had the guts to go for it until now! My first coding experience was with a singular course in university and I loved the process and that it felt like an intersting puzzle.

When I started working, I tried to apply as many of my skills as possible to automate my tasks and loved doing that as well. I love helping out my team by fixing any of their problems and keeping manual work to a minimum.

I found Bloc when I was researching crash courses and it appealed to me because I could fit it into pockets of time throughout my days and whenever I need to on a weekend. If I have a soccer game or want to go out for dinner, I can fit in my coding in these pockets while still being able to enjoy hobbies!

In whatever spare time I can find, and when we aren't in the middle of a pandemic, some of my hobbies include:

  • Traveling
  • Photography
  • Soccer
  • Dogsitting any puppy I can get my hands on!
